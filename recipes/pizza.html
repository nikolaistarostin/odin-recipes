Pizza

Read carefully now. This is a greatest secret of finest pizza!

  • Water - 200 ml
  • Salt - 1 tsp
  • Olive Oil - 2 tbsp
  • Sugar - 2 tbsp
  • Flour - 450 g
  • Live Yeast - 20 g
  • Whatever you want to put on a pizza!

Instructions:

  1. Mix water water (30-37 °C) and live yeast. Then add everything to make a dough. Do not add too much flour at once, otherwise it will be hard to mix.
  2. Put it aside for 40 minutes to rest. If dough does not expand put a pan with a hot water on top of it.
  3. Add contents of your pizza: make sure you have tomato sauce and cheese, everything else is optional.
  4. Bake in the oven for 15-20 minutes on 240°C. Bon appetit!
